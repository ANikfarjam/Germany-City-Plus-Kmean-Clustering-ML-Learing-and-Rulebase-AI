Best Universities in Germany 2023 Ranking

The 2nd edition of Research.com ranking of the best universities in Germany is based on metrics collected from Microsoft Academic Graph on 21-12-2022. Position in our global university ranking is based on total H-index values of all leading scientists affiliated with that university. A leading scientist is a researcher with a D-index (discipline H-index) above a specific threshold (usually 30 or 40) for their scientific disciplines. Our ranking of leading universities in Germany only lists 1000 universities with the highest H-index total. Show more

The ranking process involved detailed evaluation of 166,880 scientists on Google Scholar and Microsoft Academic Graph.

An indepth definition of our research process can be found on our methodology page .

Please take into consideration that the research institution or university for a scholar is typically set based on the affiliation information presented on their Google Scholar profile.

In comparison to other global university rankings our aim is to focus predominantly on research accomplishments of top scientists affiliated with each institution. We hope to inspire researchers, executives, and institutions around the world to learn where top experts are heading and to provide an opportunity for the whole research community to determine who the leading authorities in specific fields of research, in different geographies, or even within research organizations are.

The ranking process involved a detailed examination of 166,880 researchers profiles from Google Scholar and Microsoft Academic Graph. Over 143,564 profiles were examined. Scientists from more than 2,953 institutions and affiliations were analyzed.
